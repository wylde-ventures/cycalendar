The CyCal Calendar system is available in two separate, but related, formats.

Solar Year Calendar
One 365 day wheel from the Winter Solstice. Poster format.
Lunar Year Calendar
Twelve 28-30 day wheels from the Lunar New Year. Wall and book format.

Both representations contain the whole bundle (lunar months and dates, solstices and equinoxes, western dates, color-coded wheels) but each highlights this in their own way. To fully experience the CyCal system both calendar types are recommended.

2018 Solar Year Calendar

from the Northern Winter Solstice
Dec 21st, 2017 ~ Dec 20th, 2018

The Solar Year Calendar is a large-format printed 24 x 36 inch poster. The poster contains a single large color-coded calendar wheel that depicts the entire 365 day year from Winter Solstice to Winter Solstice. This solar wheel includes the Solstices, Equinoxes, and Cross-Quarters; CyCal Lunar Months, moon phases, and dates; and Gregorian months, weekdays, and dates.

Timezones - Select your local timezone to receive a calendar with lunar/solar events and dates accurately correlated with your timezone. If your preferred timezone is not listed please let me know here and it will be listed within a few days.

2018 Lunar Year Calendars

from the Northern Lunar New Year
Feb 15th, 2018 ~ Feb 4th, 2019

(exact dates of Lunar New Year depend on timezone)

The 2018 Lunar Year Calendars each contain a set of twelve circular calendar wheels -- one for each of the twelve lunar months between Lunar New Years. Lunar Year Calendars come in wall hanging, art book, and journal formats. Each of the twelve lunar month calendar pages contain a color-coded calendar wheel that depicts the 28-30 days in that lunar month. Each lunar wheel includes the CyCal Lunar Month Name; moon phases and dates; the overlapping Gregorian months, weekdays, and dates; and any Solstices, Equinoxes, or Cross-Quarters that occur during that lunar month.

2018 Lunar Year Calendars are currently in development. Availability is estimated for the first day of the Elder Winter Moon (Jan 15th).
