About Fine Art America

Fine Art America is one of the largest, most-respected giclee printing companies in the world with over 40 years of experience producing museum-quality prints. All of our prints are produced on state-of-the-art, professional-grade Epson printers.

We use acid-free papers and canvases with archival inks to guarantee that your prints last a lifetime without fading or loss of color. Paper prints include a 1" white border around the image to allow for future framing and matting. Canvas prints include a 2.5" white border to allow for future stretching on stretcher bars.

Prints ship within 3 - 4 business days in durable cardboard tubes.

Print Options

If you intend to use it as a calendar (want to be able to write on it)

You should choose one of the non-glossy papers. The following papers can all be easily written on with pen or pencil.

Premium Archival Matte Paper
Non-textured, cool-toned, high-contrast
Cool Tone Picture Rag
Slightly-textured, cool-toned, heavy-weight
Somerset Velvet
Textured, warm-toned
Watercolor Paper
Textured, cool-toned

More detailed info on the papers can be found by clicking the '?' when selecting a paper.

I don't mount or frame my calendar prints as I want to be able to roll them up and store them after the year is over.

If you don't need to write on it

The world is your oyster -- you can choose whatever paper you prefer and configure the mat and frame to look great on your wall. I still prefer the non-glossy prints, but if you want to go glossy, the Semi-Matte Photo Paper and the Metallic Paper produce the best prints for my eye.
