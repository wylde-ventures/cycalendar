Lunar/Solar Circular Calendars

CyCal Calendars combine a custom lunar/solar calendar system with the Gregorian calendar system and present the result in unique, wheel-based, color-coded calendar and journal designs.

Click here to purchase 2019 Calendars!

The CyCal Calendar system is presented in two separate, but related, formats.

Solar Year Calendar
Highlights the solar year with a single calendar wheel from Winter Solstice to Winter Solstice.
Lunar Year Calendar
Highlights the lunar year from Lunar New Year to Lunar New Year with twelve or thirteen lunar month calendar wheels.

Both representations contain the whole bundle (lunar months and dates, solstices and equinoxes, western dates, color-coded wheels) but each highlights this in their own way. To fully experience the CyCal system both calendar types are recommended.
