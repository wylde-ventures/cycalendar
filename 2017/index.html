Cyclic Calendars

Delightfully designed to sync up your schedule with natural rhythms.

CyCal's cyclic calendars are modeled directly on the rhythmic cycles of nature and are made to inspire the harmonic scheduling of life’s events.

Using calendars based on natural cycles helps to reorient our contextual perspective of time.

When we start scheduling in this way our sense of where we are in time can become rhythmically oriented to the natural cycles that are all around us.

It's natural to start noticing the correlations in "felt sense" of morning, the waxing moon and spring; of midday, the full moon and summer; of evening, the waning moon, and autumn; and of night, the new moon and winter.

When starting to orient to these naturally occuring pulses and rhythms of energy and experience you might start to see them everywhere.

How deep does the rabbit hole go?

The rhythms of the Sun and Moon are the syncopated metronomes in the symphony of life.

As cliche as it sounds, this pulse is nothing more, and nothing less, than the great circle of life. It is birth and death. It is life and compost.

We can - and we must - rock out on our own solos from time to time. But if we do so without resonance with the underlying rhythm - isn’t that just obnoxious noise?

Let's regain our awareness of the basic pulses inherent in the life around us and keep the beat with the rhythms of the world.
